Kobolds!

Kobolds are a fictional race from the game Dungeons and Dragons. Typically a xenophibic and cowardly species though they tend to be industrious. found in many parts of the world though prefering dark and damp caves, perfect places for them to build their many traps. It is believed they have close ties to dragons. The species goes as far as to worship dragons as gods. more specifically the dragon Tiamat and Io are depicted as gods of creation
